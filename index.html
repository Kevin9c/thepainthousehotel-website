Our Offers

Executive Suites

Starting from N 700k

An executive room caters to business travelers' needs, providing a comfortable workspace and business-oriented amenities like high-speed internet, a work desk, and conference call facilities. These rooms often come with access to an executive lounge or exclusive business services.

Luxury Suites

Starting from N 400k

The suite is a luxurious and spacious option, providing a separate living area and bedroom. Suites are designed to offer a more indulgent experience, often featuring a king-size bed, elegant decor, and premium amenities. Guests staying in suites typically receive added benefits and personalized services, making them ideal for special occasions or VIP travelers.

Deluxe Suites

Starting from N 200k

The deluxe room offers an upgraded experience with more space and additional amenities. Guests can enjoy a larger bed, plush furnishings, and enhanced in-room facilities. These rooms often come with a minibar, coffee/tea maker, and a work desk, making them suitable for both leisure and business travelers.

Standard room

Starting from N 100k

The standard room is a comfortable and well-appointed option for guests seeking a relaxing stay. It usually comes with essential amenities like a comfortable bed, a private bathroom with basic toiletries, and a television. Ideal for solo travelers or those on a budget who are looking for a cozy space to rest.

Family Room

Starting from N 300k

Family rooms are specifically designed to accommodate families or larger groups. They offer ample space, multiple beds, and sometimes extra features like a sofa bed or connecting rooms. Family-friendly amenities may include children's play areas, games, and kid-friendly entertainment options.

Poolside Room

Starting from N 200k

Poolside rooms offer direct access to the hotel's pool area, allowing guests to enjoy the pool conveniently. These rooms are perfect for guests who love swimming or simply want to soak up the sun by the poolside.
